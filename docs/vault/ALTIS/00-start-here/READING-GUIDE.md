1. Comprehensive Roadmap (sections 1–4)

3. Marketing Strategy Draft
4. Market Pulse plan

1. Brand Strategy

## First-week schedule

| Day | Read | Outcome |
|-----|------|---------|
| Day 1 | Comprehensive Roadmap | Know the 6 tracks and phase order |
| Day 2 | Brand Strategy + Verbal Identity | Consistent messaging |
| Day 3 | Market Pulse plan (sections 1–20) | Pre-launch funnel clear |
| Day 4 | Website Master Plan (sections 1–12) | Page priorities clear |
| Day 5 | Business Model + Business Plan summaries | Revenue and ops understood |
| Week 2+ | Product chain (if building) | Ready to implement |
