# Nano Banana 2 Prompts - Altis Website Images

## 1. Purpose

This document contains a complete **Nano Banana 2 prompt library** for generating the image system needed for the Altis website.

It is based on:

- the Altis brand strategy and visual identity
- the Altis website master plan
- the Nano Banana 2 prompt system

The prompts are designed for a **premium boxing and fitness brand website** and are optimized for:

- homepage hero images
- section visuals
- program imagery
- membership visuals
- blog and social-compatible website graphics
- open graph images
- supporting textures, abstract visuals, and gallery images

## 2. Non-Negotiable Generation Rules

Use these rules for **every single generation**.

- **Always attach the current official Altis logo image as a reference asset**
- **Use the attached logo as the exact official logo reference**
- **Do not redesign, reinterpret, distort, redraw, simplify, replace, or invent a new logo**
- **Preserve the logo’s core proportions, geometry, and brand recognition**
- **No persons in any image**
- **No human silhouettes**
- **No body parts**
- **No hands**
- **No athletes**
- **No coaches**
- **No crowds**
- **No faces**
- **No figurative human presence at all**
- Use **objects, architectural space, equipment, signage, materials, motion cues, abstract forms, lighting, textures, and branded environments** instead
- Keep the visual result **premium, minimal, disciplined, modern, and commercially usable**
- Avoid cheap sports aesthetics, neon overload, aggressive fight-poster energy, and visual chaos
- Prefer **clean layout discipline**, **luxury restraint**, and **high-end editorial composition**
- Avoid long visible text inside the image
- If text is needed, keep it extremely minimal and ideally limited to the attached logo only

## 3. Strategic Visual Summary

### Brand Position
Altis is a premium modern boxing and fitness brand built around discipline, transformation, elevated performance, and structured progress.

### Desired Feeling
- disciplined
- elevated
- modern
- premium
- controlled strength
- serious but not aggressive
- athletic minimalism
- strong without chaos

### Visual Territory
- premium athletic minimalism
- dark editorial sports luxury
- architectural and material-rich environments
- refined performance energy
- no macho clichés
- no noisy gym aesthetic

### Core Palette
- **Midnight Black** `#0B0D12`
- **Ivory Mist** `#F3EFE7`
- **Deep Crimson** `#A61E2D`
- **Burnished Bronze** `#8C6A43`
- **Steel Gray** `#6B7280`

### Typography Feel
- geometric modern sans
- clean grotesk
- premium editorial hierarchy
- minimal text presence

### Allowed Visual Ingredients
- branded walls
- boxing ring ropes without people
- heavy bags without people
- gloves and wraps as objects only
- floor texture
- brushed metal signage
- matte black surfaces
- ivory editorial layouts
- crimson highlight lines
- bronze foil details
- architectural light and shadow
- abstract motion lines
- rising geometry
- disciplined grid systems

### Forbidden Visual Ingredients
- fighters
- people training
- portraits
- stock lifestyle photos
- cartoon style
- cheap gym poster look
- cluttered scenes
- fake AI anatomy
- crowd scenes
- dramatic blood, sweat, violence, or fight-night cues

## 4. Recommended Workflow

Use the prompts in this order:

1. Generate the **concept system boards** first
2. Lock the visual direction
3. Generate the **hero images**
4. Generate the **section visuals**
5. Generate the **program-specific visuals**
6. Generate the **blog / OG / utility assets**
7. Generate the **gallery and texture assets**
